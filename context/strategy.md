## Current Focus Period

12 months — targeting income doubling and efficiency transformation without team growth.

## Strategic Priorities

1. **Double income** — Grow from ~$117k to ~$234k annually ($10k/month → $20k/month)
2. **Improve delivery efficiency** — Reduce time on repetitive tasks through better systems, templates, and AI automation
3. **Scale without hiring** — Grow revenue through leverage (better packaging, smarter workflows, higher client value) not headcount

## What Success Looks Like

- Monthly revenue at or approaching $20k
- Workload feels manageable — same or fewer hours, higher output
- Time freedom and flexibility are still intact
- Family commitments and travel are not compromised
- The business runs more systematically — less reliant on Tonya doing everything manually
- Enquiry flow has improved and conversion rate from enquiry to client is stronger

## Key Strategic Constraint

The main bottleneck: **how to grow revenue without increasing workload in a way that reduces flexibility.**

This currently shows up as:
- Delivery relies heavily on Tonya directly
- Too many custom, one-off tasks eating into capacity
- Low enquiry volume (1-2/month) limiting growth ceiling
- Conversion from enquiry needs improvement

## Growth Model

Growth must come from:
- AI and automation reducing time on admin, reporting, content, and proposals
- Stronger client packaging and potentially higher-value retainers
- More efficient onboarding and delivery workflows
- Improved positioning and credibility to attract better-fit clients
- Potentially improved lead generation beyond referrals

## Key Decisions / Open Questions

- How to package services for higher average client value without more delivery time
- Where to invest time to get the highest leverage (AI systems vs. marketing vs. new offers)
- Whether to create any productised or semi-scalable offerings
- How to improve enquiry flow beyond referrals and Unicorn Factory
