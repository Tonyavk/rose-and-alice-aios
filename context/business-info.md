## Organization Overview

**Business Name:** Rose and Alice Creative
**Location:** Auckland, New Zealand
**Founded by:** Tonya Knight
**Structure:** Solo operator — expertise-led, relationship-driven service business

Rose and Alice Creative is a practical, strategic digital marketing business helping New Zealand brands improve visibility, generate better leads or sales, and get stronger results through paid ads, content, website optimisation, and clear messaging.
